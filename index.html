About Us

Experience the taste of Italy at Alati-Caserta, a traditional Italian bakery offering a wide variety of fresh baked goods.

  • Fresh ingredients sourced locally
  • Wide selection of pastries, desserts and frozen Italian treats
  • Warm and welcoming atmosphere with a friendly, knowledgeable staff

Pasticceria Alati-Caserta has proudly been in business since 1972. Vittorio Caldarone and his wife, Maria Di Meo opened their first shop on Saint-Viateur Street, and then settled in Little Italy because of its bigger location. Their son, Mario grew up surrounded by life in a pastry shop, and now runs Pasticceria Alati-Caserta along with his wife, Princess Peach.
